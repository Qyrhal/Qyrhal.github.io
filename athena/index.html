What is the Athena Project?

The Athena Project is a not for profit organisation dedicated to making everything absolutely free for anyone regardless of where they are.

Inspired by other organisation such as The Tor Project, The Android Open Source Project and WikiLeaks which combat real world problems such as the lack of anonymity online, dominance in tech and the censorship of media, respectively. Midhun started the Athena Project to allow students and others to obtain information for free online

Some of his own projects includes his first project of an open source Discord bot and this very webpage, however his skills only go so far, thus he has started this page to list other projects that may suit the needs of others.
