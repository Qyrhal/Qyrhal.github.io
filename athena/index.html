What is the Athena Project?

The Athena Project is a remarkable open source initiative that aims to provide free and unrestricted access to information for everyone, regardless of their location or financial status. It is a non-profit organization that was founded by Midhun, who was inspired by other organizations like The Tor Project, The Android Open Source Project, and WikiLeaks that strive to tackle real-world problems. Midhun's vision behind The Athena Project is to create a platform where students and individuals can access information and resources for free. The project comprises a range of open-source projects, including a Discord bot and a website that lists other projects suitable for various needs. The Athena Project's mission is to break down the barriers that prevent access to information and promote a world where knowledge is free and available to all. The team behind this project is committed to contributing to a more open and accessible internet that empowers people to explore, learn and create without restrictions.
